Beginner volunteering

Not sure how to start? Don't worry. You can contribute your time in a number of ways. We routinely call and gather feedback from our beneficiaries. We enquire whether they faced issues, whether our field staff was helpful and whether they had any feedback for us, helping us assess ourselves.

The best part is, you can perform these phone-audits!. Write us an email with subject: Volunteer-Disability care and mention your phone number in the body. We'll get in touch right away.
